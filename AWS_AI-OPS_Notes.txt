ML models can be used for a wide range of tasks, including classification, regression, clustering, and dimensionality reduction. They can be trained using a variety of techniques, such as supervised learning, unsupervised learning, and reinforcement learning.

There are many types of ML models, including linear models, decision trees, support vector machines, neural networks, and many others. The choice of which model to use depends on the nature of the problem you are trying to solve and the characteristics of the data you have available.
